1. Introduction

Welcome to IMM Marketing Hub. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our social media management application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Social Media Account Information

  • Access tokens for your social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn)
  • Account names and IDs
  • Page and organization information

2.2 Content Data

  • Posts and content you create through our app
  • Media files you upload
  • Scheduling information

2.3 Usage Data

  • How you use our application
  • Posting history and analytics
  • Error logs and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Social Media Posting: To post content to your connected social media accounts
  • Account Management: To manage your social media connections
  • Content Scheduling: To schedule and publish your posts
  • Analytics: To provide insights about your social media performance
  • Support: To help you with technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • Access tokens are encrypted and stored securely
  • We do not share your personal data with third parties
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information

5. Social Media Platform Integration

Our app integrates with the following social media platforms:

  • Facebook: We access your Facebook Pages to post content and read engagement data
  • Instagram: We access your Instagram Business account to post content
  • LinkedIn: We access your LinkedIn Company page to post content

Each platform has its own privacy policy and terms of service that you should review.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in our app
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Revoke access to your social media accounts
  • Export your data
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

7. Data Retention

  • Your data is stored locally and remains under your control
  • When you delete your account, all data is permanently removed
  • Access tokens are automatically refreshed according to platform policies

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
